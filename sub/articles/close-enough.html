Close enough

When floating points are just annoying

have you ever wanted to just compare to floats, with enough precision ? I am proud to show close_enough.hpp 


    

        close_enough will automatically find the best epsilon to use for your numbers at compile time. No need to fiddle around with weird epsilon values std::abs or std::numeric_limits<T>::epsilon().

        It's header only, licensed under MIT and really just a tiny piece of code. I
        t requires c++17.
